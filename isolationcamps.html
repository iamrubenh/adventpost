Isolation Camps to be built in Canada Published October 10, 2020 - By Ruben Hernandez (Concentration Camps in Europe, Source: Unsplash)

Canada plans to build isolation camps from coast to coast not limited to COVID-19 Patients.

Lawmaker Randy Hiller in Ontario Parliament: “Yesterday I asked this government if the people of Ontario should be prepared for internment camps. In September the Federal Government posted a call for expressions of interest, for contractors to supply, provide and manage quarantine/isolation camps throughout every province and every territory in Canada. These quarantine/isolation camps however are not limited to people with COVID, but provide a wide latitude for many people to be detained. Surely this government is aware of the intentions to build these isolation camps from coast to coast.”

It seems to be that these camps will be used for a wide range of things and people that are not specifically identified in the documents.

Could these be used as concentration camps for people?
