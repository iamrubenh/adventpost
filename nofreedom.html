Freedom of Speech but no Freedom to Protest against Catholicism Published October 31, 2020 - By Ruben Hernandez (Pope Francis in Congress of the United States, Source: Getty Images)

Pope Francis says there are limits to freedom of speech, especially when it comes to someone's religion, in comments that made reference to the deadly attack last week on the French satirical magazine Charlie Hebdo. Francis defended freedom of speech, calling it a fundamental human right, but said it must not cause offense. – Pope Francis backs freedom of speech - but within limits, NPR.ORG
